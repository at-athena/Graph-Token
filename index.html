Roadmap

Phase 1: Genesis

1. Project Inception (Q1):
- Team assembles and defines the vision for GRT.
- Initial community outreach and seed funding.

2. Development Kickoff (Q2):
- Smart contract development begins.
- Formation of partnerships with early data providers.
- Community building and awareness campaigns start.

Phase 2: Building the Foundation

3. Testnet Launch (Q3):
- GRT testnet goes live, allowing developers to experiment with indexing and querying.
- Bounty programs and developer grants initiated to encourage participation.

4. Mainnet Release (Q4):
- Launch of the GRT mainnet.
- GRT becomes a functioning part of the blockchain ecosystem.
- Data providers and users onboarded.

Phase 3: Expansion

5. Developer Ecosystem Growth (Q1):
- Introduction of developer tools and libraries for easy integration.
- Focus on expanding the GRT developer community.

6. Decentralized Governance (Q2):
- Implementation of governance mechanisms allowing GRT holders to participate in decision-making.
- Community-driven proposals and voting begin.

Phase 4: Adoption and Integration

7. Partnerships and Integration (Q3):
- Partnerships with major blockchain projects and applications for GRT integration.
- Expansion into various blockchain ecosystems.

8. User-Friendly Interfaces (Q4):
- Development of user-friendly interfaces to facilitate easy data querying and indexing for non-technical users.

Phase 5: Long-Term Sustainability

9. Scalability and Optimization (Ongoing):
- Continuous development to optimize GRT's performance and scalability.
- Research on layer 2 solutions for improved efficiency.

10. Community and Ecosystem Growth (Ongoing):
- Focus on building a thriving GRT community and enhancing data provider networks.
- Regular updates and improvements based on community feedback.
